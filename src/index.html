About Radiant Apparels

Established in 1994, Radiant Apparels is a Government-recognized Export House engaged in the manufacturing and export of ready-made woven garments. With an annual production capacity of over 2 million pieces, we serve clients across Europe, the USA, and the Asia-Pacific regions.

Our Core Strength

We specialize in woven bottom wear for babies and children, consistently meeting the evolving demands of global brands with a strong commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. Our long-standing partnerships, spanning nearly three decades, are a testament to our reliability and trustworthiness in the global apparel industry.

Trusted by Global Brands

We are proud to manufacture garments for some of the world’s most respected brands, known for their commitment to quality and style.
