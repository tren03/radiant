Precision in Every Stitch

Established in 1994, Radiant Apparels is a government recognized export house, delivering high-quality woven garments to Europe, USA, and Asia Pacific regions.

Who We Are

We specialize in woven bottom wear, babies and children’s wear. Our ISO 9001-compliant production systems and 100% online fabric inspection ensure top-tier quality.

With a capacity of over 5 million garments per year, we have built long-term relationships with our clients through consistency, quality, and a commitment to excellence.

Business Unit Overview

Our business unit is focused on operational excellence, continuous improvement, and synergy. We embrace teamwork, accountability, and responsiveness as core values.

We produce garments for global brands like PETIT BATEAU, TBS, and domestic labels such as US POLO and ELLE under Arvind Lifestyle Brands.

SA 8000:2014 Certification
